This app helps city commuters and tourists easily rent bikes by allowing them to quickly find, reserve for 10 minutes, and unlock bikes using their phones. Users can track their ride, pay digitally, view ride analytics like calories burned and carbon footprint saved, and save favorite stations. The app also includes features for bike owners to list their bikes and implements penalties for incorrect parking. The goal is to provide a fast, reliable, and easy-to-use bike rental experience while encouraging eco-friendly transportation.
